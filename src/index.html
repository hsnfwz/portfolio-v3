who am I?

Hey! My name is Hussein and I am a Full Stack Developer based in British Columbia, Canada with 3 years of industry experience and over 5 years of web development experience.

I absolutely love designing and building amazing user interfaces and user experiences and aspire to work on innovative real-world projects to provide value to both consumers and businesses.

I enjoy volunteering with my community organization that I founded back in 2023, which has since reached almost 700 members! I collaborate with my team to plan and execute various social events throughout the year, and spend most of my time as Public Relations Director, reaching out to community members to promote our events as well as be available for any questions and feedback.

I love to spend my free time watching hockey and cheering on the Vancouver Canucks, playing volleyball and paddleboarding with my friends in the summers, weight lifting at my local gym, keeping up with blockbuster movies and binge-worthy shows, and reading about technology, business, and politics.

why hire me?

I love working with people from various backgrounds and expertise levels towards a common goal and continuously learning to improve myself and my team members.

I am a problem solver who is always ready to put in maximum effort when researching, brainstorming, and implementing ideas to complete tasks within set deadlines. I also love immersing myself with web technologies, tinkering with new projects and concepts, and tracking the latest trends in the tech industry to boost my productivity.

If you are looking for a hard worker who can work individually and be a team player by communicating effectively, learning quickly, contributing ideas, and embracing company culture and values, then I am your guy!
